Plant Disease Detection Tool

Our advanced AI-powered detection tool helps you identify plant diseases quickly and accurately. Simply upload a clear image of the affected plant part, provide some basic information, and get instant results along with customized treatment recommendations.

  • Identifies over 50+ common plant diseases affecting various crops
  • Provides precise pesticide dosage based on land area and actual infection spread
  • Suggests organic and chemical treatment options
  • Offers prevention tips to avoid future outbreaks
  • Maintains history of your previous detections for reference

The earlier you catch a plant disease, the easier it is to treat. Don't wait until your entire crop is affected - detect and treat at the first sign of symptoms!
